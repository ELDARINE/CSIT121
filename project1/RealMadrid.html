Real Madrid C.F.

History

Real Madrid Club de Futbol is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain.

The club was founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club and has since then become one of the most successful football clubs in the world.

Real Madrid has won the UEFA Champions League/European Cup a record 14 times, the most recent being in 2022.

The club has also won the La Liga title a record 35 times, the Copa del Rey 20 times, the Supercopa de Espana 12 times, the UEFA Super Cup 5 times, and the Intercontinental Cup 3 times.
