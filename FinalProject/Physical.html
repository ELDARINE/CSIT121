Social & IRL games

Both social and IRL games underscore the importance of personal interactions, promoting face-to-face communication, teamwork, and the creation of shared memories. In an era dominated by digital connectivity, these games provide a refreshing and tangible way for people to come together and enjoy each other's company.

Example of the games

Football

football combines elements of strategy, athleticism, and teamwork, with players specializing in various positions to execute intricate plays. The sport has a massive following, with the National Football League (NFL) being one of the most-watched sports leagues in the country.

Karaoke

Karaoke is not just about vocal performance; it's a social activity that brings people together for shared musical enjoyment and often serves as a centerpiece for celebrations and social events.
